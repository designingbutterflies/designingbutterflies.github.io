Top 2 Most Scenic Places in Thailand

Click here for more photos!

Railay Beach

It is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in Thailand. To get to the beach, one must take a long-tail traditional boat from Ao Nang. Aside from enjoying the sparkling water, one can also rockclimb on the limestone cliffs, raft and kayak, or go hiking to Diamond Cave. No matter your interests, there is something for everyone.

Koh Phi Phi

The Phi Phi islands are a collection of islands and beachs that can be reached by kayak or boat fromthe mainland. Some of the most popular beaches to visit are Monkey Beach and Long Beach The beaches are beautiful, with their clear blue water and pristine sand.
